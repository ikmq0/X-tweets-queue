        How to Use

        1. Verify Connection: Click "Check Connection" to ensure your bot is properly authenticated with X. If it fails, update your X_AUTH_TOKEN in the .env file.

        2. Configure Settings: Use the Settings button to define your daily posting windows (e.g., 08:00, 12:00, 18:00). The app will randomly add a few minutes of "jitter" to these times so posts look natural.

        3. Queue Tweets: Write your tweet and click "Add to Queue". The app will automatically calculate the next available time slot based on your settings.

        4. Manage Queue: You can Edit or Remove queued tweets at any time. If a tweet fails to post, it will appear in the "Failed" section where you can Retry or Dismiss it.

        Note: If the server is turned off, any missed tweets will automatically be shifted to future windows when you turn it back on.
